Our Story

Born from Tradition,
Crafted with Love

Saffron House was founded in 1998 by Chef Arvind Sharma, who left his home in Lucknow carrying nothing but his grandmother's recipe book and an unshakeable belief that food is the most honest form of love. Today, 26 years later, that same spirit infuses every kitchen we run.

We source saffron directly from Kashmir, grind our masalas fresh every morning, and use only free-range proteins. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure, honest Indian food — the way it was always meant to taste.

  • Hand-ground fresh spices every morning
  • Authentic regional recipes from 6 Indian states
  • No artificial colours, flavours, or preservatives
  • Separate certified vegetarian kitchen
